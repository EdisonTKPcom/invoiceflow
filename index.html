FAQ

The compliance questions your
accountant will ask.

Is InvoisFlow actually LHDN-compliant? +
Yes. InvoisFlow is a registered IRBM middleware integrator operating under the MyInvois API framework. Every document is submitted in the required UBL 2.1 XML/JSON format with all mandatory fields including seller TIN, buyer TIN, document type, and line items. The returned UUID from IRBM is your legal proof of filing.
Which stablecoins do you support? +
Currently: USDC on Ethereum (ERC-20) and Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20). USDT on both chains. RMJDT (BNM's ringgit stablecoin, expected end-2026) will be supported on launch with 1:1 MYR parity — no FX step needed. More stablecoins and chains by user request.
How is the BNM FX rate sourced? +
We call Bank Negara Malaysia's official Open Data API to retrieve the daily USD/MYR reference rate for the exact date of each blockchain transaction. This rate is fetched at 00:30 MYT each day and stored immutably against each invoice. Your auditor can verify any rate against BNM's published daily tables.
What happens if MyInvois rejects my invoice? +
InvoisFlow displays the IRBM rejection reason in plain English (not the raw error code). For common errors like TIN mismatch or missing field, we suggest the exact fix. For schema errors, we auto-correct and retry once. If a second submission also fails, you get a notification with step-by-step instructions to resolve it manually.
Do you support self-billing? +
Yes — Agency and Scale plans include self-billed e-invoice mode. This lets you issue a self-billed document for USDC income received where the overseas buyer won't generate a Malaysian e-invoice. This is permitted under LHDN's self-billing guidelines and is common for freelancers with international clients.
What's the Stripe trial? Is there a free plan? +
Every new account gets 3 free e-invoice submissions — no credit card required. After that, you choose a paid plan via Stripe Checkout. We accept all major cards and FPX (Malaysian online banking). You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your account settings at any time. If you cancel mid-month, you keep access until the billing period ends.
